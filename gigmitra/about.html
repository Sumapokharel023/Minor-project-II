About GigMitra

Nepal's premier job portal connecting talented professionals with leading employers across the country.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talented job seekers and forward-thinking employers in Nepal by providing an innovative, user-friendly platform that facilitates meaningful career connections and drives economic growth across the nation.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and comprehensive career platform in Nepal, empowering every professional to achieve their career aspirations while helping organizations build exceptional teams that drive innovation and success.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, GigMitra emerged from a simple yet powerful observation: Nepal's job market needed a modern, efficient platform that could connect the right talent with the right opportunities. Our founders, experienced professionals in technology and human resources, recognized the challenges faced by both job seekers and employers in the traditional hiring process.

Starting with a small team of passionate individuals, we set out to revolutionize how people find jobs and how companies discover talent in Nepal. Through continuous innovation and a deep understanding of the local market, GigMitra has grown to become one of the most trusted names in Nepal's recruitment industry.

Today, we proudly serve thousands of job seekers and hundreds of employers, facilitating countless successful career matches and contributing to Nepal's economic development.
