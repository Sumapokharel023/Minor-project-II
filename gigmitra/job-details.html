Fintech Support Specialist

eSewa

Lalitpur, Nepal

NPR 30K - 50K

Per Month

Full Time On-Site Mid Level 1-2 years

Job Description

We are looking for a dedicated Fintech Support Specialist to join our customer service team at eSewa. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills and a passion for helping customers with their financial technology needs.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide exceptional customer support via phone, email, and chat
  • Troubleshoot technical issues related to mobile payments and digital wallets
  • Process customer transactions and resolve payment disputes
  • Maintain detailed records of customer interactions and transactions
  • Collaborate with technical teams to resolve complex issues
  • Stay updated on product features and company policies

Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, IT, or related field
  • 1-2 years of experience in customer service or technical support
  • Strong communication skills in English and Nepali
  • Basic understanding of financial services and digital payments
  • Proficiency in MS Office and CRM software
  • Problem-solving mindset with attention to detail

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with fintech or banking industry
  • Knowledge of mobile payment systems
  • Customer service certifications
  • Multilingual capabilities
