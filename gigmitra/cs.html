Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill in your basic information including name, email, and password. You can also upload your resume during the registration process to get started immediately.

Yes! GigMitra is completely free for job seekers. You can create an account, upload your resume, search for jobs, and apply to positions without any charges. We make money by charging employers to post job listings.

Once you've uploaded your resume, you can apply to jobs with just one click using the "Quick Apply" button. For detailed applications, click "View Details" to see the full job description and submit a customized application with a cover letter.

We support PDF, DOC, and DOCX file formats for resume uploads. The maximum file size allowed is 10MB. We recommend using PDF format for the best compatibility and formatting preservation.

When you upload your resume and complete your profile, employers can discover you through our candidate database. Make sure to include relevant keywords in your profile and keep your information up-to-date to increase your visibility.

You can enable job alerts when creating your profile or in your account settings. We'll send you email notifications when new jobs matching your skills and preferences are posted. You can customize the frequency and types of alerts you receive.
