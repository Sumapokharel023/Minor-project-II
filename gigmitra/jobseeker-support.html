Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an effective profile?

Complete all sections of your profile, upload a professional photo, write a compelling summary, and keep your information updated.

How can I improve my job search results?

Use relevant keywords in your profile, apply to jobs regularly, customize your applications, and set up job alerts for your preferred positions.

What should I do if I'm not getting responses?

Review and update your resume, ensure your profile is complete, apply to more positions, and consider expanding your search criteria.

How do I track my applications?

You can track all your applications in your dashboard. We'll notify you of any updates from employers.
