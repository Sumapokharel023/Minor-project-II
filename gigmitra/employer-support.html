Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job on GigMitra?

Simply create an employer account, click "Post a Job", fill in the job details, and publish. Your job will be live within minutes.

What are the pricing options for job postings?

Basic job posting costs NPR 5,000 for 30 days. We also offer featured listings and urgent hiring badges for additional visibility.

How can I manage applications?

Use your employer dashboard to view, filter, and manage all applications. You can also communicate directly with candidates through our platform.

Can I search for candidates directly?

Yes, you can search our candidate database using filters like skills, experience, location, and education to find the perfect match.

What support do you provide for bulk hiring?

We offer dedicated account management and custom solutions for companies with high-volume hiring needs. Contact our enterprise team for details.
